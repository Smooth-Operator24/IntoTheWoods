Our Story

Our story began with a simple belief — that nature and design can coexist in harmony. INTO THE WOODS was born from a love of the wild and a passion for craftsmanship that honors the earth. Every creation tells a story of where it came from and the hands that shaped it.

What started as a small idea under the canopy of trees has grown into a movement that celebrates sustainability, beauty, and purpose. We draw inspiration from the textures, tones, and tranquility of nature to design pieces that bring the outdoors into your home — naturally and responsibly.

At INTO THE WOODS, we’re not just creating products. We’re crafting a connection — between people, nature, and the spaces they call home.
